How to Upload to GitHub

  1. Save this file as index.html on your computer
  2. Go to GitHub.com and sign in to your account
  3. Click the "+" icon in the top right and select "New repository"
  4. Name your repository (e.g., wordle-game)
  5. Select "Public" and check "Add a README file"
  6. Click "Create repository"
  7. In your repository, click "Add file" > "Upload files"
  8. Drag and drop your index.html file
  9. Click "Commit changes"
  10. Go to Settings > Pages > Source: select "main" branch > Save
  11. Wait a few minutes, then visit https://yourusername.github.io/wordle-game

Your game will be live on the internet for anyone to play!
